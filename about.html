My Short Story

Who Am I?

Hello! My name is Andrii Morhun, and I am a front-end website developer with over ten years of experience.

During this time, I have successfully completed more than 150 diverse projects, each characterized by its execution complexity and technological sophistication. Among these are landing pages, multi-page corporate websites, online store, blogs, news portals, and various creative interactive sites.

I started my career in 2013 by developing adaptive templates for the CMS DataLife Engine, utilizing only HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and PHP. Constantly learning and evolving, he expanded his expertise to create multifunctional websites with fast loading times and smooth animations for CMS platforms like WordPress, OpenCart, Joomla, and others.

I now regularly keep up with the latest trends in development, browser updates, and new plugins to ensure that my projects always adhere to modern standards and technologies. This enables me to create not just websites, but complex, technologically advanced solutions that perform effectively across all types of devices.

My experience and skills enable me to offer clients comprehensive support throughout all stages of web development — from site concept through to launch and subsequent support.

My
Development
Approach

Fast Website Loading
I employ advanced optimization techniques including lazy loading, effective caching, and resource minification to ensure maximum site loading speed. This is critical for retaining users, as prolonged loading times can lead to visitors leaving before the page fully loads.
Responsive Design
All my developed sites have a responsive layout that display perfectly on any device, whether it be desktop computers, tablets, or smartphones. This enhances user experience and improves SEO rankings, as search engines favor websites optimized for mobile devices.
Semantic Markup Structure
Utilizing semantically correct HTML elements ensures proper content structuring. This enhances the site's comprehensibility by search engines and improves accessibility for users with disabilities.
Smooth Animation
Before implementing any animation, I carefully plan its placement and synchronization across the page to ensure a seamless interaction without delays. I use advanced techniques and solutions from leading developers, allowing for high site performance without compromising functionality.
Performance Optimization
I focus on deep optimization of site processes to ensure maximum performance. This includes adapting to server architecture, advanced caching, and using asynchronous loading of scripts and styles to minimize user wait times. These measures are aimed at ensuring stable and efficient site operation even under high loads.
SEO Optimization
I enhance website visibility by implementing essential SEO elements such as meta tags, titles, descriptions, and structured headings (H1-H6), lists (ul, ol), table, etc. Additionally, I use ordered lists and ALT attributes for images to improve indexing and accessibility. This ensures content is effectively indexed and more discoverable online.
Integration with External Services
I expertly integrate various external services into websites to enhance functionality. This includes social media APIs, payment systems, CRM systems and other third-party tools that expand site capabilities and improve user experience.
Cross-Browser and Cross-Platform Compatibility
My websites perform consistently across all browsers and platforms. By rigorously testing and adjusting for different environments, I guarantee a seamless user experience, whether accessed from a desktop, tablet, or smartphone.
Structured Data Implementation
I utilize various structured data markup formats such as Schema.org and Open Graph to enhance the website’s visibility and interaction with search engines. Implementing these markups ensures that the content is optimally presented in search results and social platforms, improving engagement and SEO performance.
Technological Mastery
I continuously refine my technical skills and knowledge to stay ahead of industry trends. This dedication to mastering new technologies ensures that the solutions I offer are not only robust but also incorporate cutting-edge functionalities that set your site apart from the competition.
Effective Communication
I prioritize clear and consistent communication with clients throughout the project lifecycle, from initial consultation to ongoing support. This approach ensures that your vision for the site is fully realized, changes are implemented efficiently, and any issues are swiftly addressed.

Treeonmars

Treeonmars is a unique website with horizontal scrolling, created for a Ukrainian team of experts who help business founders expand their operations and make their lives more successful and joyful.

The site stands out with original smooth animations and unconventional horizontal scrolling in the form of slides. Developed using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, the site also includes interactive feedback forms in the form of a test, with a PHP backend that allows sending test results to managers via email. Responsive design ensures perfect display on all device types—from mobile phones to desktop computers, switching to vertical scrolling for convenience on smaller screens.


Website Type:

Site in horizontal scrolling in the form of slides

Technologies:

HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP

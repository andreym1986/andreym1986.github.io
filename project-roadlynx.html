Roadlynx

Roadlynx, a logistics company in the United States., specializes in multimodal transportation services.

For Roadlynx, I developed two sites on the WordPress CMS: a multi-page corporate site covering all aspects of the company’s activities, and a targeted landing page for specific advertising tasks.

Both sites are distinguished by responsive layout for all devices (phones, computers, tablets), fast loading, smooth animation and interactive video backgrounds. The sites also feature feedback forms that simplify interaction with customers.


Website Type:

Corporate website with feedback forms and dynamic video backgrounds.

Technologies:

WordPress CMS, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP

Forest Manufactory

Forest Manufactory is an online store for pet furniture made from natural wood, featuring exquisite design and page changes in the form of slides.

The site captures attention with horizontal scrolling and smooth, eye-pleasing animation. Developed using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. The backend for order processing and callback forms operates on PHP. It supports responsive design and fast loading, ensuring comfortable use on all devices with a switch from horizontal to vertical scrolling for convenience on small phone screens.


Website Type:

Online store with a unique horizontal slide scrolling feature

Technologies:

HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP
